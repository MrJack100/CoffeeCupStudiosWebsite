The Basics

Phase One: If we build it, they will climb

We are making a tower (think of Trackmania's "Deep Slip" or the games "Getting Over it" and "Get to Work.") We will make some levels/floors on our end. We invite you to make some levels as well. We will choose our favorite community-made levels levels (as voted on by our 25 unpaid employees) and include them in our tower.
Rewards for having your build chosen include:

  • The chance to slap down a sign advertising your platform/content if that's your thing
  • An "exclusive" Reddit Flair honoring your contribution (Thanks u/Temporal_illusion)
  • The chance to watch people have fun with your creation.

Phase Two: Climb the Corporate Ladder

We will release the tower to the community within a couple weeks of submissions closing. The community will compete for the best time. More details on that later. Prizes include:

  • A Ficsit Coffee Mug (Purchased by us from the official Satisfactory Merch store) for the first place winner.
