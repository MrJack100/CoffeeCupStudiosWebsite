How to join a server

Forewarning, this tutorial assumes you have installed the correct mods - if you have not, please download our mod installer.

With that out of the way, please navigate to the "Server Manager" menu once in the game.

Then, click the "Add Server" button.

Enter the information found on the server list, the password will be requested shortly after.

Once you've entered the password! Click "Join" and climb the Corporate Ladder!

If you need any further assisstance, or are encountering issues: please join our discord server for support!
